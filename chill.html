Marine Drive
Mumbai
Best time to visit: 5:00 to 8:00 am
Rating: ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐ ⭐

Marine Drive, often referred to as the Queen's Necklace, is a captivating 3.6 km-long boulevard hugging the Arabian Sea. This iconic stretch is a symbol of Mumbai's charm, especially during the early hours from 5:00 to 8:00 am, when the rising sun paints the sky in hues of pink and gold. It's a favored spot for both locals and visitors, offering a serene atmosphere for morning walks and contemplation.
